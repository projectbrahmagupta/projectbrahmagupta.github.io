About

Project Brahmagupta grows out of an interest in the history of mathematics in India. Alongside their historical importance, these problems remain mathematically rich and computationally interesting.

We are currently curating the initial collection of problems and related material. The project is expected to begin on June 7, 2026.

The structure of the project is inspired in part by Project Euler and similar archives.
