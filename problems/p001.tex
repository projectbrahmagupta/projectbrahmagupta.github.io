\begin{problem}{Vargaprakriti}
The vargaprakriti --- ``square-nature'' --- is Brahmagupta's name for the equation $x^2 - N y^2 = 1$ in positive integers, studied in the Brahmasphutasiddhanta (628 CE). His method of composition, bhavana, generates new solutions from any known one.

Find the smallest positive integer $x$ for which $x^2 - 92 y^2 = 1$ admits a positive integer solution $y$.
\end{problem}
