\begin{problem}{Madhava’s \(\pi\)-Series}
    Consider the series:
    \[
    \pi=\sqrt{12}\left(1-\frac1{3\cdot3}+\frac1{5\cdot9}-\frac1{7\cdot27}+\cdots\right),
    \]
    whose terms decrease geometrically. Using the alternating-series error bound, determine the smallest number of terms required to guarantee that the resulting approximation differs from \(\pi\) by less than \(10^{-10}.\)
\end{problem}
