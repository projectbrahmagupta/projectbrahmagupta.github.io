\begin{problem}{Harder–Narasimhan polygons.}

    An increasing lattice path uses unit steps right \((1,0)\) or up \((0,1)\), and we define its slope as the total number of up-steps divided by the total number of right-steps. A path from \((0,0)\) to \((n,n)\) is called \textit{semistable} if the slopes of its maximal constant-slope segments never increase.
    How many \textit{semistable} lattice paths from \((0,0)\) to \((2000,2000)\) are there?
    \end{problem}
